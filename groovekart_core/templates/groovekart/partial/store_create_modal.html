How To Connect Your GrooveKart Store:

  1. Go to the login page and enter credentials of your store: https://my.groovekart.com/
  2. Select "Enter Back Office" option for your GrooveKart Store and you will be redirected to back office of your store.
  3. After successfully logging in to your store's back office, select the Find Products link under Products & Categories section in left menu.
  4. After the page has loaded, look for the "Dropified" app and click Learn More.
  5. Once the app has loaded, click on the "red color" button to activate the app.
  6. Upon successful activation of app, click App Settings, you will see the API Key and Auth Token appear.
  7. Copy and paste the API Key and Auth Token into their respective fields in Dropified.

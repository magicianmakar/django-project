How To Connect Your GrooveKart Store:

  1. Go to the login page and enter credentials of your store: https://my.groovekart.com/
  2. Select "Enter Back Office" option for your GrooveKart Store and you will be redirected to back office of your store.
  3. After successful login to store back office, select App Manager link under APPS section in left menu.
  4. After page is loaded, go the bottom of the page look for "Dropified API Credentials" app.
  5. Once app is located on the page, click on the "red color" button to activate the app.
  6. Upon successful activation of app, you will see the API Key and Auth Token in the app box right above the activation button.
  7. Copy and paste the API Key and Auth Token into their respective fields in Dropified.
